Here are the fonts which are used in the official template.

The subfolders contain the complete font packages for each font:
- Baskervald ADF Std
  Available at http://arkandis.tuxfamily.org/adffonts.html
- Gillius ADF nÂ°2
  Part of the Gillius Collection at http://arkandis.tuxfamily.org/adffonts.html
- Bitstream Vera Sans Monospace
  Available at http://ftp.gnome.org/pub/GNOME/sources/ttf-bitstream-vera/1.10/
- Share
  Available at http://news.typo3.org/news/article/share/

To install these fonts copy the OTF and TTF files from the packages
into the Fonts folder of your operating system.
E.g. on Windows this folder most commonly is located at C:\Windows\Fonts.
